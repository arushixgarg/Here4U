Our Aim

Here4U is a mental health counselling website. Here4U was founded Â in order to help in providing mental health resources and support to individuals anonymously and in a secure way in India. We offer online counseling services to help individuals cope with stress and anxiety, manage depression, and improve self-esteem. We are committed to providing quality mental health services to individuals in Lucknow and beyond. We also offer a variety of educational materials on topics related to mental health, such as anxiety, depression, self-care, and more. Our goal is to create a safe and supportive environment for individuals to access the resources and support they need to thrive.

Our Misssion

Here4u is to provide accessible and reliable resources for individuals seeking support for their mental health. The website aims to destigmatize mental health issues and promote awareness of the importance of seeking help when needed. It provides a range of resources, including articles, blogs, forums, and access to mental health professionals, to support individuals in managing their mental health and well-being. Additionally, the website may provide referrals to local mental health services or resources for those who require more intensive support. Link
